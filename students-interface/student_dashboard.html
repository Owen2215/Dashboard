💡 Understanding Your Skills

🎯 Major Skills Each department defines 9 specialized competencies central to that field. Example: Accounting tracks Financial Reporting, Auditing, Taxation, etc. Your mastery here shows your depth in your major.
🌐 General Skills (9 universal) PK Professional Knowledge — auto-computed as the average of your major skills (your overall major depth).
QA Quantitative Analysis — math, stats, modeling.
DI Data Literacy — data collection, analysis, visualization.
CM/IP/ER/TL/IC/SL — communication, innovation, ethics, teamwork, intercultural, self-directed learning.
How scores are computed: Each course's outcome / syllabus text is matched against keywords for every skill dimension. The score combines coverage (how many keywords match × 65%) + depth (how often they recur × 20%) + specificity (whether the skill is in the course title × 15%). Result is rounded to 0–100.
